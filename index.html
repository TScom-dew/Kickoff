Why Visit Hazaribagh?

Lush forests, wildlife sanctuary, tranquil dams, and a calm town vibe — perfect for short nature getaways and wildlife enthusiasts.

  • Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary & National Park — large forest reserve with diverse fauna.
  • Tilaiya / Tilaiya Dam — scenic reservoir and picnic spot.
  • Surajkund Hot Spring and natural spots (local attractions).
  • Connected by road and rail to nearby cities; regular buses and taxis available.

Quick Facts

Hazaribagh
  • Sanctuary area: 184–186 km² (Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary / National Park).
  • Tilaiya Dam: major reservoir on the Barakar River, popular picnic spot.
  • Access: Connected by road to Ranchi, Gaya, and others — buses and taxis available.
